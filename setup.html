Welcome to Halfsies

A shared expense tracker for two people.

This wizard will walk you through setting up your own instance. You'll need:

  • A Google account
  • ~10 minutes

At the end, you'll download a config.js file that makes Halfsies yours.

Step 1: Create a Firebase Project

  1. Go to Firebase Console
  2. Click "Create a project"
  3. Name it anything (e.g. "halfsies")
  4. Disable Google Analytics (not needed) and click Create
Why do I need Firebase?

Firebase provides the database (Firestore) and sign-in (Google Auth) that Halfsies uses to sync expenses between two people in real time. The free tier is more than enough.

Step 2: Enable Google Sign-In

  1. In your Firebase project, go to Authentication → Sign-in method
  2. Click Google and toggle it on
  3. Pick any support email and click Save
